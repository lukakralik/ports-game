User Manual

Welcome to Ports!

Ports is a strategic trading game where you manage crews, trade goods, and compete to become the most successful trader. Below is a guide to help you navigate the game.

1. Admin Panel

The Admin Panel is where you can initialize and manage the game. Here's what you can do:

  • New Port: Create new ports with unique prices for goods like rice, tea, wine, spices, gold, diamonds, and cannons.
  • Active Ports: View and manage all active ports in the game.
  • New Crew: Create new crews with unique names, colors, and starting balances.
  • Active Crews: View and manage all active crews in the game.
  • Game Timer: Start a countdown timer. When the timer ends, the game is over, and no further actions are allowed.

2. Ports

Ports are the heart of the game. Each port has different prices for goods. Here's what you can do:

  • View Port Details: Click on a port to see its prices and available goods.
  • Trade Goods: Buy or sell goods at ports to maximize your profits.

3. Crews

Crews represent your trading team. Each crew has a limited carrying capacity and a starting balance. Here's what you can do:

  • Manage Crews: View your crew's inventory, balance, and carrying capacity.
  • Trade Goods: Use your crew to buy and sell goods at ports.
  • Become a Pirate: If your crew buys cannons, they become pirates and can steal from other crews.

4. Pirates

Pirates can steal from other crews. Here's how it works:

  • Steal Funds: Pirates can attack other crews and steal a percentage of their balance.
  • Cannon Advantage: The crew with more cannons wins the battle. If both crews have the same number of cannons, the attack fails.

5. Services

A multitude of services can be managed by the port operators.

  • Loans: Invidual crews can take out loans. The interest rate and amount limit is determined by the port operator. The loan can be reapaid in multiple transfers or at once.
  • Crew storage upgrade: For an amount determined by the port
  • Arbitrary transfers: The operator is allowed to perform any kind of fund transfer for visiting crews if he deems it appropriate. Free play is expected but not enforced.

6. Game Over

When the game timer ends, the game is over. A "Game Over" screen will appear, possible options are New Game and Results screen. The winner is determined by the crew with the highest balance and most valuable inventory.
