Service offer

Loan

Crews are permitted to take out loans. Loan size, interest rate and maturity time is completely determined by the port operator. This implies the operator needs to keep a track of time with a separate timer. Debt repayment is facilitated through "Arbitrary transfer". Unpaid debt at the end of the game results in instant bankrupcy of the given crew.

Capacity increase

All crews start the game with the default storage size of 3 items. This number can be increased up to 5. Upgrade price is determined by the operator.

Safe deposit

At any point of the game all of the crews are allowed to deposit some portion of their balance to the bank.
